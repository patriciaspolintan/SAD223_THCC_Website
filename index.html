Project Lupang Pangako

The Holy Cross Columbarium caters is an ideal sanctuary for remembering your dearly departed. THCC is one of our ways to gather funds and realize Project Lupang Pangako, a plan that aims to acquire the vacant lot situated in front of The Parish of Nuestro Señor Jesucristo and the Diocesan Shrine of Saint Andrew Kim Tae-gon, and be part of its growing community.

Our Services

What We Offer

Discover our essential offerings.

  • Ash Depository

    a dignified resting place for cremated remains, providing space for up to two regular-sized urns in a single unit.

  • Memorial Service

    A heartfelt gathering to honor the life of a departed individual, it provides an opportunity for reflection during a time of loss.

  • Internment

    Placing an urn into a niche deserves recognition, much like placing a casket into the ground. It marks the final step in saying goodbye.
